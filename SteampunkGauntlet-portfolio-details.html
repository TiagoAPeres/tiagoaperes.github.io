Description

With the task of creating a 3D steampunk inspired model, I decided to create a gauntlet inspired by Lord of the Rings and Batman's Bane. The gauntlet would inject the wearer with a drug (as seen in the vials) to make them stronger and faster. The user could use the handwheel and dial to control the flow of the drug.
