Description

In RevoGuard, social status is decided by duels between 2 people equipped with shields and revolvers, where the winner will increase their rank in the social hierarchy.

Playing as a citizen of RevoGuard, you will fight to rise in power by engaging opponents in fast-paced duels with a focus on quick reaction times as well as creating strategies, using the environment to your advantage.

My responsibilities

  • Character movement and shooting 
  • Level/arena design 
  • UI (including menus) and effects (Niagara system) 
  • Basic online multiplayer integration
