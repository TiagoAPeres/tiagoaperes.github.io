Description

AfterSky is a 3D single player first-person, action adventure platformer.

In a futuristic world where major seismic activity destroys a gravity machine, causing parts of the land to float to the sky, use your skills as a parkour athlete to repair the machine and bring the world back together.

AfterSky is heavily focused on precise movement and traversal, with enemies being sparse. The player must use various movement options such as sprinting, climbing, sliding, and wall running to traverse the floating islands, collect the gravity machine parts and fix the broken planet.
